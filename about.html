What defines us

Our values define the OVest culture, who we are, and what we do every day. They are the foundation of our identity and the compass of our interaction with all stakeholders: customers, regulators, investors, partners and our communities.

Customer Satisfaction
Obsession

We are in business to make our customers happy and meet their needs. Meeting the needs of our customers is an act we value dearly.

Collaboration &
Teamwork

Our success depends upon the collective intelligence of our team members and the shared vision of our partners.

Open & Honest
Communication

Do it right. Say and share the right thing. We manage tough situations with courage and candor. We welcome healthy debates and differences of opinion.

Innovation
& Leadership

We lead by doing the best work. What we do, we do very well. It is our nature to innovate and take responsibility for our actions.

Personal Growth
& Development

Our growth as a company is a product of the growth of our individual team members. Grow daily. It compounds. Do your best work, daily.

Simplicity
& Empathy

We create simple but powerful experience because greatness is simple. We see with the eyes of our customers, listen with their ears and feel with their hearts.
